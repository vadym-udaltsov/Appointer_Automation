APPOINTER

Say goodbye to endless phone calls and missed appointments with Appointer!
   Our system, built on the Telegram bot, completely automates the process of booking cosmetic procedures. With just a few clicks, clients can easily find available time slots on their desired date and with a specific specialist. Additionally, they can easily manage their appointments, cancel when necessary, and access important information about the salon. Salon administrators have full control over services, available dates, and customizable workflows.
Experience the convenience and efficiency of Appointer right now!

Key Features of Appointer

Appointment without calls and messages
Salon information for clients
Full appointment details for admins
Ability to create days off
Ability to create appointments by phone
User blocking
Sending messages and posters to clients
Notification of appointment creation and cancellation
Links to salon's social networks for clients
Ease of sharing among clients

How It Works

Be sure to follow our video instructions
  • 1

    Step 1

    Create a bot and get a token
    • Find the BotFather bot in Telegram
    • Use the command /newbot to create a new bot
    • Give your bot name (visible to your clients) and then a unique username (it should end with "_bot," for example, @my_salon_bot)
    • After successfully creating the bot, BotFather will provide you with a token
  • 2

    Step 2

    Register
    • Click the "Start Working" button
    • Click the "Register" button
    • Enter your email and click "Verify email"
    • After the popup message appears, go to your email, find the email from Amazon Web Services, and click the confirmation link in it
    • Click the "Register" button
    • Fill out the password form fields and click OK
    • Find the email with the confirmation code in your mailbox, paste the code into the form, and click OK
  • 3

    Step 3

    Create a salon and configure it
    • Click the "Create Salon" button
    • Enter the name (visible only to admins), time zone, and your bot's token
    • Click "Create"
    • Change the specialist's name from "owner" to your name
    • Create at least one service
    • Click "Update Salon" and fill in the working hours, daily booking limit, and weekend days
    • You can start using the bot
  • 4

    Step 4

    Demonstration of Admin Features
    • Admin capabilities:
    • Create appointments on behalf of clients (if the client does not want or cannot use the bot)
    • Delete these appointments (admins cannot delete appointments made by clients in the bot)
    • View the list of appointments on a selected date
    • Create, view, and delete days off
    • Block and unblock clients
    • Send messages and images to all clients
    • Create notes for each client
    • Set the salon's location, create a salon description, and create links to your social networks
    • Don't forget to add to the calendar the entries you already have scheduled for when you start working with the bot
  • 5

    Step 5

    Demonstration of Client Features
    • Client capabilities:
    • Create an entry for themselves
    • View their records
    • Cancel their entries
    • View information about the salon (location, description, links to social networks of the master)
